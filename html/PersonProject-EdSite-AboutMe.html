Meet Edward

It all started with a Miter Saw

After trekking through the grueling process of looking for and buying a home, Ed and his wife thought they could finally start the "fun stuff" and decorate.
However, they came to realize that a lot of peices were too expensive for the quality they were offering. After saying "man I could make this" for the millionth time, Ed decided that he would start on the adventure of woodworking.
